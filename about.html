About Gloves Up

Our Mission

Gloves Up Boxing Gym was founded to provide a safe haven for young people in vulnerable communities. What started as a small initiative to keep kids off the streets has grown into a movement of mentorship, discipline, and empowerment. Through boxing, we teach life skills, resilience, and self-belief.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a network of youth boxing hubs across Cape Town, each one becoming a beacon of safety and transformation. We believe in building communities one child at a time, showing that with guidance and opportunity, tomorrow can look very different from yesterday.

Meet the Founders

Kelvin

Once caught in the pull of gangs and juvenile detention, Kelvin turned his hardships into purpose. Today, he leads Gloves Up with passion, showing children that they can rise above their circumstances.

The Team

Alongside Kelvin stand four other coaches, each shaped by difficult beginnings. Together, they mentor, coach, and protect, committed to ensuring that no child feels alone in their struggle.
