Join the Gym

We’re a youth-first boxing community. Kids and teens get priority access to safe, structured training, mentorship, and life skills. Adults are welcome on a fitness track that supports the mission and models discipline for younger boxers. If cost is a barrier, speak to us about sponsored places and loaner kit.

  • Kids (8–16)

    Foundations in stance, guard, footwork, and respect. We mix movement games, pad work for focus, and short huddles about discipline and safety. Consistency first: 2–3 sessions a week beats any single “big” day.

  • Adults — Fitness

    Technique + conditioning built on real boxing skills: bag rounds, pad work, mobility, and intervals. Scaled for all levels — the aim is sustainable progress and great cardio while supporting a youth-led gym.

  • Advanced / Sparring

    Ring craft, timing, defensive layers, controlled sparring, and comp prep. Entry requires solid fundamentals and coach approval for safety. We build decision-making and composure, not just power.

Volunteer with Purpose

We don’t do broad “help wherever” roles. We plug skills directly into what the kids need each week. Pick a lane below and we’ll set you up with a clear checklist and a time block.

  • Assistant Coach (Youth) — shadow for 2 weeks, support warm-ups, pad rounds, stations, and safety supervision. Minimum 1–2 sessions/week. Requires safeguarding orientation and coach approval.
  • Homework Club (Math/English/Reading) — 60–90 minutes after school, small-group support, tracking a simple goals sheet so learners don’t fall behind academically.
  • Safeguarding & Admin — attendance logs, consent forms, parent comms, check-in/out. Keeps the gym safe and organised while coaches coach.
  • Media & Storytelling — capture short clips/photos, write 2–3 captions a week, and post progress updates. We’ll provide brand guidelines and a content sheet.
  • Kit & Facilities — clean, label, and repair gloves/wraps; set up stations before classes; reset the space after. Light DIY welcome.
